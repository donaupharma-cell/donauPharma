Who We Are

Namibia's Premier Contract Pharmaceutical Manufacturer

Donau Pharmaceuticals is a purpose-built pharmaceutical blister packaging and contract manufacturing company, established in Windhoek in 1997. We are a 100% Namibian-owned business serving public sector buyers, government tender programmes, and international pharmaceutical partners entering the Southern African market.

Our facility is designed and operated in accordance with FDA, EU-GMP, WHO and ICH guidelines — enabling us to deliver quality medicines reliably, at scale, with full serialisation and traceability.
