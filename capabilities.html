Safe, Affordable Medicines — From Our Facility to the Patient

Why It Matters

Manufacturing That Reaches the Patient

Every blister pack, every tablet, every serialised carton we produce is ultimately in service of a patient who depends on it. Our capabilities are built around that responsibility — ensuring that high-quality, GMP-compliant medicines reach Southern African patients reliably and affordably.

By manufacturing locally, we reduce lead times, cut import costs, and strengthen the resilience of regional health systems — creating a supply chain that is both commercially viable and socially impactful.

A strategic asset for national health security. Proudly Namibian.
