Regional Registration

The ZaZiBoNa Advantage

ZaZiBoNa is the Zambia, Zimbabwe, Botswana, and Namibia collaborative medicines registration initiative — providing a harmonised, materially faster and lower-cost route to multi-country market access across the SADC region through shared dossier assessments.

Rather than filing separately in each country, a single dossier submission through ZaZiBoNa enables simultaneous review and approval across all four member states — a decisive advantage for international partners entering Southern Africa.

Namibia

Medicines Control Council (MCC) — primary registration market for Donau Pharmaceuticals.

Botswana

Accessible through BURS and the SADC work-sharing dossier assessment pathway.

Zambia & Zimbabwe

Joint regional dossier review through the ZaZiBoNa initiative reduces time-to-market to months, not years.
